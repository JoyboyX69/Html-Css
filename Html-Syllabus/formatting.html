✍️ Overview of Formatting Text in HTML

HTML provides several tags to format text and control how it looks on the webpage. Below are some commonly used text formatting tags:

🔤 Common Formatting Tags

  • <b> – Bold text
  • <strong> – Important (bold) text
  • <i> – Italic text
  • <em> – Emphasized (italic) text
  • <mark> – Highlighted text
  • <small> – Smaller text
  • <del> – Deleted (strikethrough) text
  • <ins> – Inserted (underlined) text
  • <sub> – Subscript text
  • <sup> – Superscript text

🧪 Example:

<p>This is <b>bold</b>, <i>italic</i>, and <mark>highlighted</mark> text.</p> Output:
This is bold, italic, and highlighted text.
<p>H<sub>2</sub>O and E = mc<sup>2</sup></p> Output:
H2O and E = mc2

👍 Tips:

  • Use <strong> and <em> instead of just <b> and <i> for accessibility
  • Combine formatting tags for better styling and meaning
