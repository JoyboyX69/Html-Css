Headings

In HTML, headings are used to define the titles and subtitles of content. They range from <h1> to <h6>, where:

  • <h1> is the most important heading (usually the main title of the page).

  • <h6> is the least important heading (used for sub-sub-sub sections). 
        
            
    <h1>This is a level 1 heading</h1>
    <h2>This is a level 2 heading</h2>
    <h3>This is a level 3 heading</h3>
    <h4>This is a level 4 heading</h4>
    <h5>This is a level 5 heading</h5>
    <h6>This is a level 6 heading</h6>
